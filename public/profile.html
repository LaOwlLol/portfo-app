Nate Gillard is an alumnus of Colorado State University. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science.

I like software engineering and programming for the unique excitement they provide me. Programming is like building a Rube Goldberg machine (here are some examples). It's like setting up a series of theoretical contraptions, and then lining them up like dominos in the dark. In the end you push over the first domino, turn on the lights, and see the resulting success or failure. I can't explain why that is so exciting. I just hope you know what I mean!
