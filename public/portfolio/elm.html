Electronic Lab Management (ELM) Notebooks:

ELM Notebooks was the product of my group's capstone project for CS464 Principals of Human-Computer Interaction course at CSU. For this project we used a contextual inquiry approach to conduct observations/interviews with laboratory biologists in a real lab setting. Later we applied user-centered design techniques to prototype an application meant to improve their workflow by eliminating breakdowns which were identified in the observation/interview stage.
